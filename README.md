## About

brillQ is an AI engineering practice building **autonomous agentic systems** — software that reasons, plans, and acts. We design and ship multi-agent architectures, LLM/RAG pipelines, and the cloud-native infrastructure that runs them, from first prototype to production-grade deployment.

We operate as a liquid organization: senior engineers and architects assembled around the work, delivering for clients across markets and sectors.

## What we build

◾ **Agentic systems** — autonomous and multi-agent architectures that orchestrate reasoning, tool use, and parallel execution toward real deliverables.

◾ **LLM & RAG** — retrieval-augmented generation, fine-tuning, and evaluation pipelines engineered for reliability at scale.

◾ **Cloud & infrastructure** — GPU-aware, cloud-native deployment, orchestration, and the operational backbone for AI in production.

◾ **Applied innovation** — turning emerging AI capabilities into working systems through rapid prototyping and production hardening.

## Beyond engineering

✦ **AI Products** — our own tools and platforms, built in the open where it makes sense.

✦ **Corporate Training** — intensive, hands-on enablement that brings development teams up to production speed on agentic AI.

✦ **Knowledge Resources** — books, courses, and technical material distilled from delivery experience.
